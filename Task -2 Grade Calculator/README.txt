# Grade Calculator

## Functionality

- **Input:** Enter marks obtained (out of 100) in each subject.
- **Total Marks Calculation:** Compute the sum of marks obtained in all subjects.
- **Average Percentage Calculation:** Divide the total marks by the number of subjects to determine the average percentage.
- **Grade Assignment:** Assign grades based on the average percentage achieved.
- **Display Results:** Show the total marks, average percentage, and corresponding grade to the user.

## How to Use

1. Compile: `javac GradeCalculator.java`
2. Run: `java GradeCalculator`
3. Input marks obtained for each subject.
4. Get total marks, average percentage, and the assigned grade.

## Example

### Input
Enter the total Number of students: 2
Enter the total Number of subjects: 3


Student 1
Enter the marks obtained in subject - 1: 55
Enter the marks obtained in subject - 2: 85
Enter the marks obtained in subject - 3: 100

Student 2
Enter the marks obtained in subject - 1: 95
Enter the marks obtained in subject - 2: 75
